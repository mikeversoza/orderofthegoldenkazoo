The Rules
  1. 1Each game is played over the course of one day.
  2. 2You must witness the action happening to get the point.
  3. 3You are not allowed to influence an action to happen.
  4. 4Actions can not be done on behalf of a player by a non player.
  5. 5You must black out the board to win.
